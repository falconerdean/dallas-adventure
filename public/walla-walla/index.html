Brasserie Four

French Brasserie · Wine Bar
📍 4 E Main St, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-529-2011  ·  Tue–Thu 4–9pm, Fri–Sat 11:30am–9pm  ·  Book on OpenTable
Classic French brasserie on Main Street. Upscale French fare with rotating art, high-end cocktails, and both French and Walla Walla wines. 96% recommend on 726 reviews. Open for lunch Fri–Sat. Great casual-fancy backup option.

WTF Eat It (Where There's Food)

📍 Location varies — often at Burwood Brewing near the airport 📞 541-921-1724  ·  Check Facebook / wtfeatit.com for schedule
