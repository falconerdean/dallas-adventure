Where to Eat

Local picks for breakfast, lunch & dinner — no chains, all highly rated

Bacon & Eggs

Breakfast · Brunch
📍 201 E Main St, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-876-4553  ·  Thu–Mon 8am–2pm
The go-to breakfast spot downtown. Chorizo scramble, huevos rancheros, buttermilk pancakes, shrimp & grits, house-baked pastries with Stumptown coffee. Expect a weekend line by 8am — call ahead to reserve. Perfect Saturday morning fuel before Rotie.
Breakfast Lunch Local favorite

Passatempo Taverna

Italian · Handmade Pasta · Pizza
📍 215 W Main St, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-876-8822  ·  Mon, Thu–Sun 4–9:30pm  ·  Book on OpenTable
Handmade pasta daily in a century-old building that started as a 1920s cigar shop. Wood-fired pizzas, tomahawk steak, meatballs, burrata bruschetta. Strong cocktail program. Our Friday night dinner pick.
Friday Dinner Local favorite

Saffron Mediterranean Kitchen

Mediterranean · Small Plates · Wood-Fired
📍 330 W Main St, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-525-2112  ·  Tue–Thu 4:30–9pm, Fri–Sat 2–9pm  ·  Book on Tock
Local institution since 2007. Southern European and North African flavors — flatbreads, octopus, lamb, chicken tagine, wood-fired entrees. Small and intimate (parties of 6 or less). Dog-friendly patio. Great backup dinner either night.
Dinner Backup dinner option

Brasserie Four

French Brasserie · Wine Bar
📍 4 E Main St, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-529-2011  ·  Tue–Thu 4–9pm, Fri–Sat 11:30am–9pm  ·  Book on OpenTable
Classic French brasserie on Main Street. Upscale French fare with rotating art, high-end cocktails, and both French and Walla Walla wines. 96% recommend on 726 reviews. Open for lunch Fri–Sat. Great casual-fancy backup option.
Lunch (Fri–Sat) Dinner Walkable downtown

WTF Eat It (Where There's Food)

Food Truck · Smash Burgers · BBQ
📍 Location varies — often at Burwood Brewing near the airport 📞 541-921-1724  ·  Check Facebook / wtfeatit.com for schedule
Locals call it the best burger in town. Double-smash patties with "Nay Sauce," plus pulled pork, brisket, Carolina BBQ sandwiches. Chef Nathan Carlson won 2024 Readers' Choice for Best Chef. Usually parked Wed/Fri/Sat at Burwood Brewing. Check socials before heading over.
