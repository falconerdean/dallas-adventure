Walla Walla Wine Weekend

May 1 – 2, 2026  ·  6 wineries  ·  2 dinners

Friday, May 1

Elevated + Scenic
Friday route: Caprio (south side) → Tranche (east side) → àMaurice (east side) → Downtown dinner. All stops within ~15 min of each other.
1

Caprio Cellars

11:00 AM reservation
📍 1603 Whitely Rd, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-412-3054  ·  capriocellars.com
360° valley views · complimentary food + wine pairing · sets the tone for the whole trip
Bordeaux-inspired wines paired with scratch-made seasonal plates from Chef Williams. No tasting fee — the food pairing is included. This replaces lunch. Tastings limited to 20 people, so the room stays intimate. Plan for ~90 min.
Reservation required Food pairing included
~10 min drive via Mill Creek Rd
2

Tranche

1:00 PM reservation
📍 705 Berney Dr, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-526-3500  ·  tranche.wine
Big views, structured reds · beautiful contrast after Caprio's intimacy
Known for estate-grown Bordeaux varieties and panoramic Blue Mountain views. May hours: Sun–Thu 11am–5pm, Fri–Sat 11am–9pm. Plan for ~60 min tasting.
Reservation recommended
~12 min drive east toward Mill Creek
3

àMaurice Cellars

2:30 PM
📍 178 Vineyard Ln, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-522-5444  ·  amaurice.com
Chill, pretty, slightly softer finish · ideal golden hour stop
Fri–Sat 10:30am–4:30pm. Relaxed, scenic setting with a softer wine profile than the earlier stops. Walk-ins welcome as space allows, but call ahead to be safe. Winds down by 4:30 — perfect timing to head to dinner. Plan ~60 min.
Reservation recommended
~15 min drive to downtown

🍽️ Dinner — The Kitchen at Abeja

5:30 PM seating
📍 2014 Mill Creek Rd, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-526-7400  ·  Book on Tock
Five-course prix fixe ($135/person) with optional wine pairing ($55/person). Seven-course option at $165 + $65 pairing. Chef Mike Easton's monthly menu showcases Pacific Northwest ingredients paired with Abeja wines. Seatings Thu–Sun, 5:30–7:30pm. Book early — this is a destination dinner.

Saturday, May 2

Rocks District + Experience + Social
Saturday route: Rotie (Milton-Freewater, ~25 min south) → Abeja estate (east side, ~20 min back) → Downtown walk-around. Ends where you can park once and wander.
4

Rotie Cellars — Rocks Estate

11:00 AM reservation
📍 84328 Trumbull Ln, Milton-Freewater, OR 97862 📞 509-301-9074  ·  rotiecellars.com
Rocks District terroir · rugged, earthy, distinct
Appointment times: 11:00, 12:30, 2:00, 3:30. Open daily by appointment — call ahead for same-day. $40/person tasting fee, waived with $75 purchase. This is in The Rocks District (Milton-Freewater, OR) — unique cobblestone terroir you can feel in the wines. Plan ~75 min.
Reservation required
~20 min drive north to Mill Creek Rd
5

Abeja Winery — Estate Tasting

1:00 PM reservation
📍 2014 Mill Creek Rd, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-526-7400  ·  abeja.net
Your "moment" stop · walk the grounds, soak it in, slow down
The estate tasting room is reservation-only. Beautiful farmstead property with gardens and restored historic buildings. This is the experiential centerpiece of Saturday — don't rush it. Plan ~90 min including a walk around the grounds.
Reservation required
~10 min drive to downtown

🍽️ Dinner — Hattaway's on Alder

6:30 PM reservation
📍 125 W Alder St, Walla Walla, WA 99362 📞 509-525-4433  ·  Book on Resy
Southern-inspired fine dining with house-cured meats, local seafood, and a great local wine list. Sat hours 4–9pm. Intimate downtown spot — walkable from your last tasting. Pairs perfectly with a day of big reds: think smoked meats + rich Walla Walla Syrahs.
